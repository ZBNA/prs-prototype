You can apply for a direct payment of rent online

You can do this if you are a Landlord and have a tenant who is in rent arrears and they receive Universal Credit. It will tell you how to ask for rent to be taken direct from their benefit and paid to you.

You'll be given the form to download and the email address to send it to.

Before you apply, you will need your tenants postcode.
