Use this tool if you are a Landlord and have a tenant who is in rent arrears and they receive Universal Credit. It will tell you how to ask for rent to be taken direct from their benefit and paid to you.

This tool is to help you find the right form to request a direct payment, and to send it to the correct place.

You will need your tenants postcode.
