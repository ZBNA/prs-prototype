Apply for a direct payment
of rent

Only use this tool if you're a landlord and your tenant is receiving Universal Credit.

The tenant's rent will be paid directly to you from their benefit.

You can apply if the tenant has:

  • at least 2 months rent
  • mental health problems
  • addiction problems
  • has recently left care
  • bad credit

You'll be given the form to download and the email address to send it to.

You'll need to enter the postcode they are living at (or will be moving into).
