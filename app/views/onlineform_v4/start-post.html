You can apply for a direct payment of rent, or rent arrears deduction if you're a landlord.

You can ask for rent payments to be made direct from your tenant’s Universal Credit if they’re having difficulty paying you.

You'll be given the form to download and the address to send it to.

Before you apply, you will need your tenant's postcode.
