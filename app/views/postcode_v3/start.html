Request direct rent payments if you're a landlord

If a tenant is in rent arrears of 2 or more months you can ask for payments to be taken direct from their Universal Credit.

You can ask for regular direct payments of rent (known as ‘managed payments’), deductions for rent arrears, or both.

You will need to complete and send a form to the Department for Work and Pensions.
