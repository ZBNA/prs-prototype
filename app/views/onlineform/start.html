Apply for a direct payment of rent

Use this service if you're a landlord and your tenant is getting Universal Credit.

You can ask for the following to be paid directly from their benefit:

  • regular monthly rent
  • re-payment of arrears or third party deductions
  • payment for temporary accommodation

You'll need to give your bank details to use this service.

Before you start
